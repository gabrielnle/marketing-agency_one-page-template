Description

What can I do with the Tutoshop¬¥s plugin

Live Commerce or Shopstreaming, call it what you want! LiveComm Tutorshop is a plugin that brings the physical shopping experience to the online world. You interact with your customers in real time, via live and chat, and sell your products and/or services on the same screen.


The shopper watches the live on its website, interacts via chat, and can buy the products presented without having to leave the live screen, as the selected goods are displayed below the live, with a purchase option.


Made for any type of store and category: cosmetics, clothes, services, games, bazaar, whatever you want!


Sell more by interacting with your customers and facilitating the sale of your products.


This is the first version. It's a beta version, please give us your feedback so we can improve.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions

  • What is shopstreaming?

    Shopstreaming means using live streaming technology to make online sales . In this way, while a salesperson is showing and talking about a certain product, the consumer can interact, ask questions and, in a few clicks, complete the purchase.

  • Are you a streamer?

    No, we mirror your live video using the main streams, so you record from your twitch and/or facebook account and the video appears in real time on your platform.

  • And how does the chat work?

    The person can either send you questions through your website screen, or directly in the streams, we compiled everything in a single chat, but the ideal is always to call all customers to just one channel of communication, yyour website, where they can buy the products right below the live.

  • How do I select the products that I want to appear on the live?

    You create a category, like any other and add the products you want to your live, we advise you to call the created category ‚Äúlive‚Äù or something similar, it is important that you edit the category every time you do a different live, if you want to change the products.

  • How do I schedule the live?

    Click on Dashbord ü°™ Settings ü°™ LiveComm Tutorshop, it will open a screen for you to add your live commerce. You must fill in the fields listed on the live screen, which are the same below:
    1. Stream title = live title
    2. Stream Platform = Choose which stream you want to use, twitch or facebook
    3. Live Url = livestream link
    4. Product Category = Category created with the products for the live
    5. Live date = Livestream day and time

  • How do I mirror Twitch video on the platform?

    Just schedule a live normally through the Twitch platform, and register your user on your e-commerce when registering the live. For example, if your username is: LINDO123, just enter LINDO123 in the Live Url tab when registering the live.
